Collaborate with RCC

RCC develops projects that benefit from interdisciplinary collaboration, specialized methods, public or existing datasets, and coordinated operational support. We welcome proposals from researchers, clinicians, analysts, independent scholars, institutions, and prospective project partners.

Bring a question

Propose a research question that requires expertise beyond a single discipline or laboratory.

Bring a dataset

Identify an existing or public dataset that could support a novel, rigorous, and mission-aligned analysis.

Bring a capability

Contribute scientific, clinical, computational, methodological, operational, or humanistic expertise to an emerging project.

Propose a collaboration

To propose a collaboration, email contact@religiouscognitioncollab.org. Share as much or as little as you like — the more you can include, the faster we can respond.

Helpful details to include:

  • Your name and institution or independent affiliation
  • A short description of the proposed project or contribution
  • The research question, if you have one
  • Any available dataset or resources
  • The expertise you bring, and the expertise you are seeking
  • Funding status, if relevant

You are welcome to attach a concept note.
