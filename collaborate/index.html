Collaborate With Us

RCC is built for research questions that benefit from expertise across disciplines, institutions, and traditions. We welcome serious inquiries from researchers, institutions, students, and technical specialists interested in developing or contributing to rigorous work on religion and cognition.

Ways to work with RCC

For researchers

Researchers may approach RCC with a project concept, a dataset, a methodological need, or an interest in joining an existing project. Productive inquiries identify a clear question, the expertise already available, and the expertise still needed.

For institutions

RCC welcomes project-based relationships with universities, research centers, laboratories, nonprofit organizations, archives, and data repositories, with a defined purpose and clear expectations regarding oversight, funding, data, authorship, and communication.

For students and early-career researchers

Opportunities may include research assistance, coding, literature review, data preparation, analysis, project coordination, and collaborative writing, depending on active projects and supervision capacity.

In an inquiry

What to include

  • Your name, institution, and current role.
  • Your research interests and relevant expertise.
  • The project, question, dataset, or collaboration you are proposing.
  • The specific contribution you can make or support you are seeking.
  • Any relevant timeline, funding opportunity, or deadline.

A public inquiry channel for the Collaborative is being established and will be posted here.
