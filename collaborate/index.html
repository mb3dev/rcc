Collaborate

Build the right team around the question.

RCC works with researchers, institutions, data stewards, students, and funders on projects that fit its scope and benefit from interdisciplinary coordination. A strong proposal begins with a clear question, a realistic path to data or participants, and an honest account of the expertise still needed.

Research collaboration

Bring a defined question, theory, dataset, or study concept that would benefit from additional expertise or an organizational home.

Data and methods partnership

Work with RCC on secondary analysis, quantitative design, neuroimaging, EEG, longitudinal modeling, computational methods, or reproducible workflows.

Student and early-career participation

Contribute to a defined project through supervised analysis, literature review, data preparation, coding, or research communication.

Start a conversation

Write to research@religiouscognitioncollab.org. A strong inquiry describes the question you want to pursue, the kind of collaboration you are seeking, and what data, participants, funding, or approvals already exist. We respond if the proposal appears to fit RCC's scope and current capacity.
