Outputs

Manuscripts, analyses, and proposals produced through RCC projects. Peer-reviewed publications, preprints, code, and data links will be added here as they become public.

In preparation and under review
Proposal under review Faith, Healing Expectation, and Neuroplasticity in Parkinson’s Disease A proposed precision-mapping study examining whether sustained Christian healing expectation is associated with within-person changes in neural function and corticostriatal connectivity. Manuscript in preparation Religious Socialization and Neural Organization A large-scale secondary analysis testing whether religious upbringing, current religiosity, and religious importance are associated with brain morphology or shared neural responses during naturalistic viewing. Manuscript in preparation Psilocybin and Default Mode Network Connectivity A within-subject secondary analysis testing whether oral psilocybin acutely reduces internal default mode network connectivity, and whether an eight-week mindfulness program alters that effect.

Peer-reviewed publications, preprints, posters, datasets, and code repositories will be listed here as outputs become public.
