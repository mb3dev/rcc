RCC brings together researchers whose expertise fits the needs of specific projects. The Collaborative has continuing organizational leadership, while investigators, advisors, analysts, and institutional partners may participate in one or more projects.

Founding Director

Michael Barros

Michael Barros is the founding director of the Religious Cognition Collaborative and a PhD candidate in psychology whose research focuses on grounded cognition, dreams, religious experience, and the cognitive science of religion. His work combines quantitative analysis, theory development, and interdisciplinary scholarship. He directs the Collaborative’s research agenda, develops new projects, assembles project teams, and oversees research operations.
