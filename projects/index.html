Projects

Projects

RCC organizes its work through defined research projects. Each project has a focused question, a designated leadership team, an appropriate methodological plan, and a clear set of anticipated outputs, while the Collaborative provides a continuing framework for project development, coordination, and dissemination.

How projects are formed

From question to completed work

Projects may begin with an investigator, a dataset, a funding opportunity, or a research problem that requires expertise across fields. RCC helps define the question, identify the necessary team, establish roles, develop the plan, and carry the work through to publication and dissemination.

Current project details will be published here once they are approved for public release.
