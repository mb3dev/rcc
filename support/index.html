Support

Support Research

Independent research requires more than strong ideas. Participant compensation, data collection, software, analysis, research assistance, publication, and collaboration all require material support. Contributions help RCC develop and complete rigorous projects that might otherwise remain unfunded.

Ways to support the work

General Research Fund

General support allows RCC to develop new projects, respond to emerging opportunities, and cover shared research costs that are not tied to a single restricted award.

Project Support

Donors and funders may support an approved project with a defined purpose, budget, leadership team, and anticipated outputs.

Institutional and Foundation Funding

RCC welcomes conversations with foundations, institutions, and philanthropic partners interested in supporting rigorous research on religion, cognition, consciousness, and human experience.

Where it goes

What support may fund

  • Participant recruitment and compensation.
  • Data access, collection, storage, and preparation.
  • Research software, analytic tools, and technical services.
  • Research assistants, analysts, and project coordination.
  • Travel, scholarly meetings, and collaborative workshops.
  • Open-access publication and public dissemination.
