Support

Support research on religion and the mind.

Private support can make early-stage research possible before a project is large enough for conventional grant funding. RCC seeks support for pilot studies, participant costs, data access, analysis, open-science infrastructure, research assistance, and publication.

Current priorities

Where support goes

  • Pilot and proof-of-concept studies
  • Secondary analysis of open datasets
  • Neuroimaging and EEG analysis
  • Student and early-career research participation
  • Open data, code, and research resources

A supporting nonprofit, designation process, and receipt language are being finalized before contributions are invited. Details, including tax and designation information, will be posted here.
